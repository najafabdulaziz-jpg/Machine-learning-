## Dataset Description
The Blood Transfusion dataset contains information about blood donors, including recency, frequency, total blood donated, and time since first donation. It includes 748 records and 5 features. The dataset was obtained from Kaggle and is suitable for classification tasks.

## Machine Learning Problem
This is a binary classification problem. The goal is to predict whether a donor will donate blood again in March 2007 based on their past donation behavior. The target variable is the "Class" column, where 2 represents a donation and 1 represents no donation.
